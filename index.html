A Place of Compassion & Growth

At Temecula Valley Counseling and Mindfulness Center, we believe that everyone deserves access to high-quality, compassionate mental health care. Our team of experienced therapists is dedicated to walking alongside you — wherever you are in your journey.

We integrate evidence-based therapeutic approaches with mindfulness practices to support lasting, meaningful change.

  • A non-judgmental, confidential, and culturally sensitive environment
  • Personalized care plans grounded in evidence-based practice
  • Mindfulness-informed approaches to healing and growth
  • Flexible scheduling, including telehealth options
