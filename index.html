A Message from Our Director

Welcome to TVCMC

From the moment you walk through our doors you will know we are different. Our hope is that you will feel welcome and at home.

We are located in Murrieta, which allows us to be close to wherever you live. While many therapy practices today offer only virtual appointments, we love to see you in person — especially children, who we believe do best with face-to-face connection. We also understand that busy schedules, younger children, and life's challenges sometimes make in-person visits difficult, so we offer virtual appointments as well. Whether in-person or virtual, you will have the same kind and caring experience with your therapist.

At TVCMC you will find a variety of caring therapists, both male and female, with different areas of expertise. All of our therapists have experience working with children and adults dealing with depression, anxiety, and trauma. Read about our specialties on the Therapists page.
